Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan. Add the onions, garlic and carrot.
  2. To make the white sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan, stir in the plain flour, then cook for 2 mins.
  3. Oven-roast the aubergines for 25 mins
  4. Spoon the remaining white sauce over the pasta, making sure the whole surface is covered, then scatter over the mozzarella and cherry tomatoes.
